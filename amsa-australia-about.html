About AMSA Australia

We are a student-led, not-for-profit, non-political organisation connecting Australian medical students with the world.

Who We Are

Asian Medical Students' Association (AMSA) Australia Chapter is part of AMSA International, a peak representative organisation for medical students from across Asia, the Asia-Pacific, and beyond.

AMSA trains future doctors-to-be to share knowledge, undertake inter-cultural activities and social services, and create international friendships. As the Australian Chapter, we serve as the bridge connecting Australian medical students with their peers across Asia and beyond.

Established as a platform for cultural exchange and professional development, we offer Australian medical students unique opportunities to engage with the international medical community through exchanges, conferences, and collaborative projects.

Our Mission & Values

Knowledge Sharing

We facilitate the exchange of medical knowledge and innovations across borders, enhancing the educational experience of future healthcare professionals.

Cultural Exchange

We promote understanding of different healthcare systems and practices through immersive experiences and collaborative learning.

Global Leadership

We develop leadership skills in medical students, preparing them to address healthcare challenges with a global perspective.
